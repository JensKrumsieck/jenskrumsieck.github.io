BluEPRint

BluEPRint is a viewer for Bruker EMX Spectrometer files (.par/.spc) with easyspin-Integration (ESPlug).
Features

  • View and analyze EPR spectra
  • Mark g-values and measure hyperfine couplings
  • calculate absorption (integral of signal) and 2nd derivative (derivative of signal)
  • spin quantification (TODO)
  • Multiple exports
  • Integration of easyspin with ESPlug (GUI for easyspin, MatLab Installation with easyspin needed)
